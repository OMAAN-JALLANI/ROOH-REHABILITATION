Our Treatment Philosophy

  • Reintegration Planning

Spiritual Healing

  • Self-Awareness
  • Inner Growth & Purpose
  • Emotional Resilience
  • Mindfulness Sessions

Our approach integrates all four dimensions to ensure a sustainable recovery. We don't just treat the addiction; we heal the Rooh (Soul).
