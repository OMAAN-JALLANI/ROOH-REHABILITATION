1. Psychological Services

Comprehensive assessment, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of psychological and psychiatric conditions. Each client receives an individualized treatment plan tailored to their specific needs, ensuring structured, ethical, and compassionate care.

Conditions Treated

  • Depression
  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Stress and Adjustment Problems
  • Trauma and PTSD
  • Personality-related difficulties
  • Behavioral issues
  • Emotional regulation problems
  • Psychosis
  • Schizophrenia
  • Mood disorders
  • Adolescent and adult mental health concerns

Our Services

  • Detailed psychological assessment
  • Individual psychotherapy
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Supportive therapy
  • Family counseling
  • Psychoeducation
  • Crisis intervention (when required)

2. Addiction Treatment

We offer structured and comprehensive treatment programs for both substance-related (chemical) and behavioral (non-chemical) addictions.

Chemical Addictions (I)

Opioids:
  • Heroin
  • Opium
  • Morphine
  • Codeine
  • Tramadol
  • Fentanyl
  • Oxycodone
  • Methadone (misuse)
Alcohol:
  • All forms of alcohol dependence

Chemical Addictions (II)

Cannabis & Related:
  • Marijuana
  • Hashish
  • Synthetic cannabinoids
Stimulants:
  • Cocaine
  • Crack cocaine
  • Amphetamines
  • Methamphetamine (Ice)
  • MDMA (Ecstasy)

Chemical Addictions (III)

Sedatives & Depressants:
  • Benzodiazepines
  • Sleeping pills (Z-drugs)
  • Barbiturates
Hallucinogens & Inhalants:
  • LSD / Psilocybin / Ketamine
  • Glue / Petrol / Paint thinners
  • Nitrous oxide

Non-Chemical Addictions

  • Internet addiction
  • Gaming addiction
  • Gambling addiction
  • Pornography addiction
  • Social media addiction
  • Shopping addiction
  • Food-related compulsive behaviors

Focus: Identifying triggers, restructuring patterns & long-term recovery.

Treatment Includes

  • Medical detoxification (when required)
  • Inpatient rehabilitation
  • Individual and group therapy
  • Relapse prevention planning
  • Family counseling
  • Aftercare and follow-up programs
